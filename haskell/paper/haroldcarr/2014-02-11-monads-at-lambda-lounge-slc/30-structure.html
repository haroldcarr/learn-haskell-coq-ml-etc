composing logging

Want to compose f' . g' — but inputs/outputs do not match, so adapt: 

let (y,s) = g' x
    (z,t) = f' y in (z,s++t)

generalize (factor out composition)

bind :: (Float -> (Float,String)) -> ((Float,String) -> (Float,String))
bind f' (gx, gs) =
    let (fx, fs) = f' gx
    in  (fx, gs++fs)
